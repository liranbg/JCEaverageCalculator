jceManager 1.0.0
====================

Compile with QT

  Launch, insert your JCE username and password.<br>
  Once your logged in, go to GPA Tab and hit "ADD"<br>
  Now you will see all your courses in a table content<br>
<br>
  Edit your grade and see the average difference.<br>
